Fire Alarm Systems

We offer a wide range of residential, commercial, and industrial fire alarm systems. Our safety solutions encompass fire and carbon monoxide detection, alarm signaling, incident management, emergency evacuation, and certified monitoring networks to protect lives and assets.

Addressable Control Panels & Certified Incident Evacuation

MNC (MAJAL) specializes in supplying, installing, and commissioning advanced addressable fire alarm control panels, detectors, and modules. Our systems are engineered to integrate seamlessly with emergency voice evacuation (EN 54 certified), building automation, and HVAC controls. In case of a fire or gas leakage incident, our centralized panels coordinate automatic shutdowns of dampers, elevator recalls, and electromagnetic door releases to establish safe evacuation paths.

From a safety and security standpoint, our fire systems also sync with building access control, event monitoring, electronic locks, and video management grids. This ensures safety personnel receive real-time, pinpoint alerts on security consoles and mobile applications, helping them coordinate responses swiftly and minimize property damage.

Certified Heat Detectors, Call Points & Sounder Strobes

We specialize in end-to-end design, supply, installation, commissioning, and annual maintenance (AMC) of complete fire detection setups.

Our product range includes certified fire alarm control panels, smoke and carbon monoxide detectors, manual call points, sounder strobes, modules, repeater panels, and diagnostic tools. Powered by certified fire-management software, each device undergoes rigorous testing to comply with international regulations and Saudi Civil Defense approvals, ensuring zero false alarms and 100% operational resilience.

We provide complete end-to-end design, supply, installation, commissioning, and annual maintenance support (AMC) for certified fire safety alarm systems and suppression controls across Saudi Arabia.
