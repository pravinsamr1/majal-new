Majal IT Networks & Security's Public Address & Voice Evacuation

In commercial facilities, public venues, educational institutions, and industrial plants, clear audio communication is fundamental to daily operations and critical during emergencies. Public Address (PA) and Voice Evacuation systems form the backbone of organizational communication, enabling general announcements, background music distribution, and directional evacuation messages. Majal IT Networks & Security is a leading provider of high-quality public address and voice evacuation systems across Saudi Arabia, prioritizing acoustic performance and strict compliance with global safety standards.

Our systems combine premium components, including digital controllers, multi-channel amplifiers, high-fidelity ceiling and wall-mount speakers, directional horns, and paging microphones. In compliance with safety regulations, such as the EN54 European standard for fire detection and fire alarm systems, our voice evacuation solutions are designed to deliver intelligible instructions during emergencies. Studies prove that voice instructions significantly reduce panic and evacuation time compared to traditional sirens, ensuring a safer and more orderly egress.

Core Capabilities of Majal Public Address Systems

We customize systems to support complex routing and multi-zone broadcasting, allowing operators to send targeted messages to specific zones without disrupting other areas. Key capabilities include:

  • Voice Evacuation
  • Multi-zone Paging
  • Alarm Integration
  • Noise Sensors
  • Horn Speakers
  • Battery Backup

Acoustic Performance & Intelligibility

Our engineers perform comprehensive acoustic modeling to measure the Speech Transmission Index (STI). This ensures that every voice broadcast remains highly intelligible even in large, highly reverberant spaces like lobbies, warehouses, and open courtyards.
