Privacy Policy

MAJAL IT NETWORKS & SECURITY (MNC - MAJAL) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (majalnet.com) or use our systems, structured cabling, CCTV surveillance, and integrated security solutions across Saudi Arabia.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you directly provide to us, such as when you submit an inquiry form, contact us, or register for support tickets. This information may include:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and physical address.
  • Inquiry Content: Messages, technical details, or project requirements submitted via forms.

Additionally, we automatically collect basic analytics data during your visit to optimize our site navigation:

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, pages viewed, and visit timestamps.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data for purposes based on our legitimate business interests, including:

  • Providing IT network, CCTV monitoring, and low-current integration service proposals.
  • Responding to client questions, submitting support tickets, and scheduling consultations.
  • Improving user experience, analytical modeling, and system security.
  • Compliance with local industrial guidelines and legal obligations in Saudi Arabia.

3. Data Protection & Safety

We apply standard administrative, technological, and physical controls to secure personal data under our possession. Access to collected personal data is strictly limited to authorized personnel who require it to serve client accounts. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may disclose data only under the following conditions:

  • To comply with legal process, national security requests, or safety guidelines.
  • To protect the rights, property, or security of MAJAL IT NETWORKS & SECURITY, our users, or public entities.
  • With service providers under strict non-disclosure terms to support our daily operations.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential and performance cookies on our website to analyze visitor traffic patterns, save custom preferences, and maintain website stability. You may opt to disable cookies via your browser settings; however, certain website features may no longer function optimally.

6. Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or complete deletion of your personal data held by us. To request data removal or query our policy, please send a message to info@majalnet.com.

7. Policy Modifications

This policy is subject to periodic updates. All revisions will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date at the bottom of the section.
