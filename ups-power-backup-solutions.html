Enterprise UPS Power Backup Systems

We specialize in the supply, installation, testing, and maintenance of Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems to protect critical network components from power drops.

Double-Conversion Racks, Battery Cabinets & ATS Panels

Data centers, servers, and security cameras must run continuously during main power outages. Our online double-conversion UPS units provide continuous, clean power, filtering voltage spikes.

We carry rack-mount UPS units for IT closets, heavy-duty three-phase UPS systems for data centers, battery backup cabinets, Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS), and metered power distribution units.

Modular Scaling Batteries & SNMP Network Cards

We integrate SNMP network cards into the UPS systems, allowing administrators to monitor power status remotely.

  • Rack-mount UPS units with hot-swappable batteries

  • UPS network cards for remote monitoring and auto-shutdowns
