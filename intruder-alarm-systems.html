Intrusion Alarm Systems

Protect your premises from unauthorized entry and vandalism with unified intrusion alarm networks. Our smart control panels are modern Internet appliances designed to easily integrate with access control, fire safety, home/building automation, and IP video surveillance.

Integrated Intrusion Detection & Video Monitoring Solutions

MNC (MAJAL) delivers comprehensive intrusion alarm systems that work in tandem with video surveillance, fire alarms, and building automation. By linking these domains together, we achieve robust facilities security, automated event-based actions, and prevention of theft or vandalism across your organization. Control operations are managed through a unified interface, lowering maintenance costs and simplifying administrative overhead.

With a single system to learn, use, and maintain, your team gets complete peace of mind. Our solutions use dual-tech motion detectors, magnetic door contacts, glass break sensors, and perimeter infrared photo-beams, coupled with GSM/IP cellular communication, ensuring emergency alerts reach your security desks and mobile devices even if primary networks fail.

IP-Based Entrance Control, Sensors & Smart Push Notifications

We specialize in deploying wired and wireless hybrid intruder alarm control panels, motion sensors, and long-range photo-electric beams.

Our setups utilize smart IP-based entrance control grids, high-security electromechanical locks, and advanced readers and controllers. Whenever an alert is triggered, our system broadcasts immediate alarms via SMS, direct calls, and mobile app push notifications, enabling rapid response to security threats. The system also supports automatic sirens and silent panic triggers connected to central monitors.

We provide complete end-to-end design, supply, installation, commissioning, and annual maintenance support (AMC) for certified intrusion detection and building security networks.
