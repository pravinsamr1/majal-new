Gate Barriers, Turnstiles & Road Blockers

Designed to operate as stand-alone equipment or part of a security network, our range of barriers, turnstiles, and road blockers can be supplied with smart technology enabling a fully integrated access control system. Suitable for both large and small premises, our automatic barriers are highly adaptable, weather-resistant, and provide maximum security and throughput.

Pedestrian Gate Access Control & Security Turnstiles

Our full-height turnstiles are specifically engineered for locations requiring pedestrian access control and high-level perimeter security. They offer comfortable passages, single or double passageway configurations, and standard roof covers with durable material finishes. These units integrate seamlessly with web-based access control systems, IP-based entrance controls, electromechanical locks, and readers/controllers.

MNC (MAJAL) ensures that all turnstiles and gates function as a key component of your facility's safety layout. By utilizing advanced smart cards and credentials, organizations can establish a fully unified access tracking network that captures pedestrian logs, tracks shift schedules, and maintains perimeter safety under all operating conditions.

Automatic Vehicle Barriers, Hydraulic Road Blockers & Bollards

We deploy automatic barrier gate operators to control parking lots and garages in commercial or industrial complexes, airports, and public facilities. These units can be operated automatically by card reader, keypad, telephone entry system, or manually controlled within a guard station or kiosk. They support one-way or two-way traffic lanes with boom arms ranging from 6' to 30' long.

For robust vehicular access control, our hydraulic road blockers (available in widths up to 4m) comply with BS6571 part 4 axle-load standards and provide exceptional physical protection. Additionally, our heavy-duty rising bollards prevent unauthorized vehicular entry and defend pedestrian areas from ramming attacks, ensuring robust protection under all weather conditions.

We provide complete end-to-end design, supply, installation, commissioning, and annual maintenance support for integrated gate barriers, turnstiles, and vehicle control perimeters.
