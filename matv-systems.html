Satellite Headends, Multiswitches & IPTV Streaming

Our systems allow properties to capture satellite television channels using central high-gain dishes, modulating and distributing them over coaxial cables or fiber networks to every room without individual dishes.

For modern settings, we deploy IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) systems, sending interactive television channels, movies, and guest services over local server IP networks directly to smart televisions.

Integrated Signal Distribution Architecture

Master Antenna Television or MATV means many TV connection outlets from one Master antenna control system. Usually high-rise apartment houses, hotels, schools and cluster based residential area utilizes this kind of TV distribution network.

The system may or may not include FM radio so that one outlet can receive both TV and FM reception.
