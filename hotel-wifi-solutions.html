Hotel Wi-Fi & Hospitality Network Solutions

We are the hospitality industry's Wi-Fi and in-room entertainment specialists. MNC (MAJAL) is a full-solution provider for secure, high-performance wired and wireless Internet services, backed by 24x7 guest service monitoring and support.

Secure Guest Wi-Fi & Beyond TV Entertainment Systems

Guests expect reliable, high-speed connectivity across the entire property. Our custom-designed Wi-Fi networks provide seamless coverage from the lobby and guest rooms to restaurants and outdoor venues. We deliver enterprise-grade equipment and services to hotels, resorts, military housing, student dormitories, timeshares, condominiums, and conference centers. Our systems are backed by proactive, round-the-clock remote monitoring, ensuring guest satisfaction is maintained at 100% under all operating conditions.

In addition to internet access, we offer advanced in-room entertainment solutions, including beyond-TV casting systems that allow guests to securely cast their favorite streaming applications directly to their room's display. All access portals are integrated with property management systems (PMS) for automated authentication, billing, and bandwidth shaping, ensuring network stability while keeping guest traffic completely isolated and secure.

Branded Captive Portals & Network Bandwidth Shaping

We specialize in deploying wired and wireless hospitality networks, secure gateways, and high-density access points.

Our setups utilize smart access controllers to implement guest isolation, bandwidth throttling, and property-wide roaming. We configure custom captive portals matching your hotel's branding guidelines, offering multi-lingual support, social login, PMS validation, and premium tier billing options. With centralized remote management, hotel administration can monitor network health and client logs effortlessly.

We provide complete end-to-end design, supply, installation, commissioning, and annual maintenance support (AMC) for certified hospitality wireless networks and guest services.
